Crash - The Adrenaline Rush

Crash is arguably the most popular game on CSGORoll. The concept is simple yet thrilling: a multiplier starts at 1x and increases continuously until it "crashes" at a random point. Your goal is to cash out before the crash happens. The longer you wait, the higher your potential winnings, but if you don't cash out in time, you lose your bet.

The game uses a provably fair algorithm that determines the crash point before each round begins. This means the outcome is predetermined and verifiable, ensuring complete fairness. Many players develop strategies around Crash, from conservative approaches (cashing out at low multipliers like 1.5x) to aggressive tactics (waiting for 10x or higher).

CSGORoll's Roulette brings the classic casino experience to the CS2 community. Dice - Pure Probability

Dice on CSGORoll is a straightforward probability game where you predict whether the roll will be over or under a certain number. You can adjust the target number to change your odds and potential payout. A higher risk (predicting a less likely outcome) results in a higher reward if you win.

This game is popular among players who enjoy mathematical approaches to gambling. You can calculate exact probabilities and expected values, making it ideal for those who prefer data-driven decisions. The game also supports auto-betting features for those who want to implement systematic strategies.

Coinflip - Head to Head

Coinflip is CSGORoll's player-versus-player game where you bet against other users in a 50/50 chance scenario. You can either create a coinflip game or join an existing one. The winner takes all (minus a small site commission), making this one of the most engaging games on the platform.

What makes Coinflip special is the PvP element. You're not playing against the house; you're competing against real players. This creates an exciting dynamic where you can choose your opponents based on their bet size. Many high-rollers prefer Coinflip for its straightforward mechanics and potentially massive wins.

Plinko - Controlled Chaos

Plinko is a visually stunning game where a ball drops through a pyramid of pegs, bouncing randomly until it lands in one of several slots at the bottom. Each slot has a different multiplier, with the edges typically offering the highest payouts and the center having lower returns.

You can adjust the risk level in Plinko, which changes the multiplier distribution. Low risk offers more consistent but smaller wins, while high risk can result in massive payouts but with greater variance. The game combines luck with satisfying visual feedback, making it a favorite among many players.
